Ski for Light, Inc. is an all-volunteer non-profit organization that teaches the basics of classic cross-country skiing to adults who are visually- or mobility-impaired. All of this is made possible by the volunteer sighted and able-bodied experienced cross-country skiers who attend as guides and instructors. But Ski for Light is much more than a learn-to-ski program... Learn more.

The 2015 Ski for Light International Week

The 40th annual Ski for Light International week will be held in Granby, Colorado from Sunday January 25 through Sunday February 1, 2015. We will stay at The Inn at Silver Creek, and ski at nearby Snow Mountain Ranch.
